Domicile Application

What is a Domicile Certificate?
A domicile certificate is an official document that proves a person's residence in a particular state or union territory. It's essential for educational admissions, government jobs, and various official purposes where proof of local residence is required.
Required Documents
  • Proof of residence (Utility bill, rent agreement, etc.)
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar card, passport, etc.)
  • Date of birth certificate
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Application form (duly filled and signed)
Application Process
1
Gather Required Documents
Collect all necessary documents as listed above. Ensure they are up-to-date and valid.
2
Fill Application Form
Complete the domicile certificate application form with accurate information.
3
Submit to Relevant Authority
Submit your application along with supporting documents to the Tehsil Municipal Administration (TMA) or relevant office.
4
Verification Process
Authorities will verify your documents and residential details. This may include a physical verification.
5
Receive Certificate
Once verified, you'll receive your domicile certificate. Processing time varies by location.
