What is a Domicile Certificate?

A domicile certificate is an official document that proves a person's residence in a particular state or union territory. It's essential for educational admissions, government jobs, and various official purposes where proof of local residence is required. This certificate is issued by the respective state government and serves as legal proof of residence.

Required Documents

  • Proof of residence (Utility bill, rent agreement, etc.)
  • Identity proof (CNIC, passport, etc.)
  • Date of birth certificate
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Application form (duly filled and signed)

Application Steps

1

Gather Documents

Collect all necessary documents as listed above. Ensure they are up-to-date and valid.

2

Fill Application

Complete the domicile certificate application form with accurate information.

3

Submit Application

Submit your application along with supporting documents to the relevant authority.

4

Verification

Authorities will verify your documents and residential details.

5

Receive Certificate

Once verified, you'll receive your domicile certificate.
