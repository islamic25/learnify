Privacy Policy

Welcome to Learnfy! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our Android application.

Important Note: Learnfy does not require user registration or login. We do not create personal user accounts or collect personally identifiable information.

1. General Summary

Learnfy is committed to protecting your privacy. Our app is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No registration or login required
  • No collection of personal information
  • Transparent about data practices
  • Compliance with Google Play Store requirements

2. Firebase Services Usage

Learnfy uses Google Firebase services to improve app functionality and user experience. These services collect non-personal, anonymized data:

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app. This helps us improve features and fix issues. The data is aggregated and anonymous.

Firebase Crashlytics

Crashlytics helps us identify and fix app crashes. It collects technical information about device specifications and crash logs, but no personal data.

Cloud Firestore & Storage

We use Firestore and Storage for app content and user-generated data (like saved preferences). This data is not linked to personal identities.

All Firebase services comply with Google's privacy policies and data processing terms.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

To be perfectly clear, Learnfy does NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Account credentials (since no accounts exist)
  • Payment information (app is free)
  • Contact lists or social connections
  • Precise location data
  • Sensitive personal information

4. Data Submitted by Users

Learnfy may store anonymous usage data and preferences locally on your device. This includes:

  • App settings and preferences
  • Progress in learning activities
  • Content preferences

This data remains on your device unless you use backup features (which would be handled by your device's backup system, not Learnfy).

5. App Permissions

Learnfy requests only the permissions necessary for core functionality:

  • Internet Access: Required to load content and use Firebase services
  • Storage (Optional): For saving content offline if this feature is available

We do not require access to contacts, location, or other sensitive permissions.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information processed by our app:

  • Firebase services use encryption for data in transit
  • Anonymous data is aggregated and cannot be traced to individuals
  • We follow security best practices in our development process

Since we don't collect personal data, the risk of personal information exposure is minimal.

7. Children's Privacy

Learnfy is suitable for users of all ages. Our privacy practices are designed to protect everyone:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • Our content is appropriate for general audiences
  • No special parental controls are needed as no personal data is collected

If you believe a child has provided any information to us, please contact us immediately.

8. Updates and Effective Date

We may update this Privacy Policy as our app evolves. Changes will be reflected here with an updated effective date.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Learnfy after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
